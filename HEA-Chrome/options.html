The extension adds the Holocene Epoch timestamp before or after the timestamps read from the webpage (or replaces them completely).

e.g.
"1 Aug. 2017" can become "1 Aug. 2017 [12017 HE]"
"399 BC" can become "/9602 HE/ 399 BC"
"10 Sep. 259 BC" can become "10 Sep. 9742 HE"
